As a top-ranked private research institution, the University of Louisana at Lafayette offers everything you are looking for: world-class resources on a human scale, more academic opportunities than any other American university, a vibrant campus life, a location in the heart of a dynamic world center and the lifelong support of the Hot Cayenne Family.
Each year more than 30,000 of the most talented high school seniors in the country and from all over the world apply to the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. We are proud of the quality, diversity and uniqueness of these applicants. We welcome you to apply.
The University of Louisiana at Lafayette offers more than 100 highly regarded graduate and professional programs that prepare students for leadership positions in research, education and professional practice.
The University of Louisiana at Lafayette administers a robust financial aid program with a long tradition of meeting 100% of the University of Louisiana at Lafayette-determined financial need for those undergraduate students who satisfy all eligibility requirements and deadlines. For graduate students, the University of Louisiana at Lafayette offers need-based financial aid, departmental grants and graduate assistantships/fellowships, along with a wide array of payment options.
